When is FTC?

Activities

How can I get involved at FTC?

There are many ways to get involved! Sign up to be a workshop host or apply to be a team captain. You can even apply to be a part of campfire skits or talent acts! Check out the apply tab for more information.

What activities are there going to be?

There will be team activities, workshops, talent acts, campfire skits, and more!

Will I be able to apply to be a SAA of some sort?

There won't be a SAA system implemented this year due to online complications. However, we will still have the SAA Buddy System, now called FTC Pen Pals, which you can apply for here!

Will there still be team activities?

Yes! There will still be team activities, so get excited for that!

Finance

How much does FTC cost?

It costs $30. It can be paid via 2 methods: Mail-in check or PayPal Transaction. Please note that if you choose PayPal, the registration cost will be $31 due to a $1 PayPal processing fee.

What does the $30 include?

The $30 includes a souvenir pack and a really amazing FTC experience!

Do treasurers need to collect money from members?

Your club treasurers will not be responsible for collecting your payments for FTC registration. You will be responsible for it yourself. Please refer to the registration form for further details on how to register on your own.

Why are we paying when it is online?

All payments are split between fundraising and exclusive FTC 2020 merch that you will receive in your souvenir pack!

Online Transition

Is FTC online this year?

Due to the nature of the COVID-19 pandemic, FTC will be happening online.

What platform(s) will FTC be broadcasted on?

FTC will be broadcasted using Zoom and YouTube Live.

Is it going to be live or pre-recorded?

It will be both live and pre-recorded. There will be some sessions such as speeches that will be pre-recorded, but things like webinars and team activities will be live.

How is FTC going to be different online compared to in-person?

We have planned online FTC to be as close as possible to the in-person FTC experience. Although it won’t be exactly the same, the experience of this event will be undeniably different, but just as memorable!
